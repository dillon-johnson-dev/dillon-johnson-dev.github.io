EQ vs IQ

What is Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Intelligence is an individuals ability to manage and navigate their emotions and perceive others. They do this by identifying, evaluating and expressing their emotions. This allows them to empathise and connect with the people around them.

How is EQ different to IQ

Both are measured in different ways. IQ is measured in an individuals skills in problem solving, logic and communicate complex ideas. EQ on the other hand is measured through the individuals ability to identify and recognize in others and yourself. That is then used to assist and navigate through your decision making.

Why is EQ important

EQ is important in building strong relationships and the ability to identify the emotions of yourself and others would be a strong asset in moments of working with teams.
